Thrift Store Information

We are a a high end thrift store with unbeatable prices and a wide array of high quality clothing, shoes, and accessories. We have many brand name items as well as a large selection of unique vintage and hipster pieces. We are a local, family-owned business that is committed to exemplary customer service, cleanliness, organization, and bringing value to the community via the quality products we provide.

Café Information

We are a family business, offering our authentic 100% Colombian coffee, pastries and food. We invite you to try our traditional dishes like our most famous La Bandeja Paisa with sweet plantains, sausage, crispy fried pork and more. We offer authentic food that is not only delicious but also reasonably portioned and priced.
